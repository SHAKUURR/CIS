Who are We?

Crow Intelligent Systems (CIS) Ltd is an integrated solutions company with an excellent background in design, development, construction, and maintenance of infrastructures for residential and commercial clients.

CIS was founded with a clear aim of providing Affordable, reliable and Sustainable Solutions ensured by continous innovation and creativity by CIS technocrats dedicated to the integration of the latest technologies including the ongoing communication challenges, interoperability and integration of automation systems, management systems, security safety systems and cloud services.

Our Mission

To provide solutions aimed at enabling organizations and individuals maximize performance, increase revenue, satisfy customers, and make and maintain profit.

Our Vision

To be the No. 1 indigenous choice company for providing affordable, reliable and sustainable engineering and technology solutions.

Our Belief
