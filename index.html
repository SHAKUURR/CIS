Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to Your Common Queries

How do smart security systems work?

Smart security systems use a combination of sensors, cameras, and connectivity to monitor your home. When an event, like motion detection or a breach, occurs, the system sends alerts to your smartphone or a monitoring center, allowing you to take immediate action.

Can I control my smart security system remotely?

Yes, you can control and monitor your smart security system remotely using our user-friendly mobile app. It allows you to arm/disarm the system, view live camera feeds, and receive alerts from anywhere with an internet connection.

How much does a smart security system cost?

The cost of a smart security system can vary depending on the system's complexity and the number of devices you require. We provide customized quotes to fit your budge

Is monitoring included in your services?

We offer monitoring services as an optional add-on. This service can provide 24/7 surveillance and alert dispatch in case of emergencies.

What is an Intelligent Building Management System (IBMS)?

An Intelligent Building Management System, or IBMS, is a centralized control and monitoring system that integrates various building automation functions. It manages and optimizes systems like HVAC, lighting, security, and energy management to enhance building efficiency and occupant comfort.

What are Integrated Power Solutions (IPS)?

Integrated Power Solutions refer to comprehensive systems that manage power distribution, backup, and control in a seamless manner. They are designed to ensure uninterrupted power supply and efficient energy utilization.
